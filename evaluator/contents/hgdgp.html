Welcome Gender and Development System (GAD System)

To ensure thorough evaluation and alignment with institutional priorities, it's essential for evaluators to familiarize themselves with the Harmonized Gender and Development Guidelines (HGDG). These guidelines serve as the framework for assessing projects and initiatives within our institution, with a strong emphasis on gender equality and inclusivity.

Key Considerations for Evaluators

  1. Familiarize Yourself with the HGDG: Before assessing any proposal, carefully review the Harmonized Gender and Development Guidelines. Understanding these guidelines will provide a basis for evaluating proposals effectively and ensuring alignment with institutional goals.
  2. Evaluate Gender Integration: Assess the extent to which gender perspectives are incorporated into the project design, objectives, methodologies, and expected outcomes. The HGDG stresses the importance of promoting gender equality and addressing the specific needs of diverse groups within the community.
  3. Check for Gender Mainstreaming: Evaluate whether the proposal incorporates gender mainstreaming principles by analyzing its potential impacts on various genders and efforts to minimize gender inequalities.
  4. Review Gender-Sensitive Indicators: Pay attention to project indicators and evaluation criteria, prioritizing those that are gender-sensitive. This indicates a commitment to assessing project impact through a gender lens and facilitates more accurate monitoring and evaluation.
  5. Consider Thoroughness and Confidence: Assess the proposal's alignment with the HGDG and the effectiveness of gender considerations. A well-integrated proposal demonstrates thorough understanding and application of guidelines, enhancing its potential for approval.
  6. Emphasize Institutional Commitment: Recognize that adherence to the Harmonized Gender and Development Guidelines reflects the institution's commitment to promoting gender equality and inclusivity. Evaluating proposals through this lens contributes to fostering a more equitable academic environment.

Thank you for your diligence in evaluating proposals in accordance with the Harmonized Gender and Development Guidelines. Should you require any clarification or assistance regarding the HGDG, please feel free to contact the Gender and Development Office for support.
